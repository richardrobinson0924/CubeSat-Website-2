Our Story

The aim of ESSENCE ( Educational Space Science and ENgineering CubeSat Experiment ) is to foster the training of highly qualified personnel (HQP) to form a critical mass of space scientists and engineers in Canada through the design, build, launch and operation of a Canadian CubeSat mission.

The key focus of the mission is centered on the training of HQP through this unique end-to-end hands-on experience, where HQP will acquire expertise in a wide variety of areas – ranging from space science and technology to communicating their work with the public to project management, and the public outreach to attract more HQP into the fields of science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM).

The ESSENCE mission will fly a 3U-CubeSat that will be designed and built by the project team, launched from the International Space Station via a US launching service provider – NanoRacks, and operated by the team via the ground station at York University. The CubeSat will be built in a 3-year period and launched into orbit in the 4th year.
